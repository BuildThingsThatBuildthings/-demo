WHO WE ARE

The Hideout Represents a New Way to Live a Golf Life in Nashville

Errol Helling

Founder & Owner

With over 20 years of experience in the golf industry, Errol's vision was to create a space where technology meets tradition. As the PGA Tour Operations and TrackMan University instructor for TrackMan Golf, he brings unparalleled expertise to The Hideout. From coaching at MTSU on Whit Turnbow's staff acting as the program's Trackman Specialist to starting Profectus Golf Acadamey Errol is Nashville golf through and through. Fun Fact he did lose his hand to an alligator while trying to save par...or was that Chubbs Peterson?

Jake Finn

Head Golf Professional

A PGA certified professional with extensive teaching experience, Jake leads our instruction program. His expertise in combining traditional teaching methods with modern technology has helped countless members improve their game. As former Head Pro at Valhalla, he brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to The Hideout Golf Club. And yes, his mustache game is always on point. Only had herpes that one time.
