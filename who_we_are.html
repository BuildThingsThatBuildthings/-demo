Our Journey

2020

The vision of The Hideout Golf Club takes shape as we identify the need for an innovative golf experience in Austin.

2021

Our first location on Sylvan Road opens its doors, introducing state-of-the-art golf technology to the Austin community.

2022

Growing community and increasing demand leads to the planning of our second location.

2023

The Hideout expands with our second location on 8th Street, bringing our unique golf experience to even more members.

Errol Helling

Founder & Owner

With over 20 years of experience in the golf industry, Errol's vision was to create a space where technology meets tradition. As the PGA Tour Operations and TrackMan University instructor for TrackMan Golf, he brings unparalleled expertise to The Hideout. From on Whit Turnbow's staff as the program's Trackman Specialist to starting Profectus Golf Acadamey Errol is Nashville golf through and through. Lost his hand to an alligator while trying to save par...or was that Chubbs Peterson?

Jake Finn

Head Golf Professional

A PGA certified professional with extensive teaching experience, Jake leads our instruction program. His expertise in combining traditional teaching methods with modern technology has helped countless members improve their game. As former Head Pro at Valhalla, he brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to The Hideout Golf Club. And yes, his mustache game is always on point. Only had herpes that one time.
